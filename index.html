Experience the Thrill

Welcome to Kaouki Kite School, your gateway to the best water sports experience in Essaouira. Our school is dedicated to providing high-quality instruction for all levels, from absolute beginners to advanced riders.

With our certified instructors and premium equipment, we ensure a safe, fun, and progressive learning environment. Whether you want to master the wind or catch your first wave, we have the perfect program for you.

How Our Lessons Work

We are a fully mobile water sports school, bringing the adventure to you.

  • Pick-up or Meet-up
    We can pick you up directly from your hotel/accommodation and drive you to the beach, or we can simply meet you directly at the spot.
  • The Best Spots
    Most of our lessons take place at the beautiful Bay of Essaouira. For exceptional cases or specific requests, we can also organize lessons at other world-class spots like Sidi Kaouki or Moulay Bouzerktoun.
  • All Equipment Included
    Travel light! All our prices automatically include top-quality equipment and appropriate wetsuits for your session.

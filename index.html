About Your Surf Instructor

With over 10 years of experience surfing the waves of Essaouira, I have dedicated my life to sharing the thrill of surfing with beginners and tourists alike.

My passion for surfing started young, and now I love teaching others how to catch their first wave in the beautiful Moroccan coast.

As a patient and experienced instructor, I specialize in making surf lessons fun and accessible for all levels, especially tourists visiting Essaouira.

How Our Lessons Work

Warm-Up

We start with a fun warm-up session on the beach to get you ready for the waves.

Theory

Learn the basics of surfing, safety rules, and wave reading in a relaxed environment.

Water Practice

Get in the water and practice catching waves with personalized guidance.

Safety First

All lessons prioritize safety, with proper equipment and constant supervision.
